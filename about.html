Who We Are

Built Different. Built to Deliver.

A Dubai-based exhibition and event company that operates like a special operations unit: small, focused, and relentless on deadlines.

Our Story

From Concept to Global Execution

(Per)Duck Events & Exhibitions was founded on a simple premise: exhibition companies are too slow, too complicated, and too expensive for what they deliver. We set out to build the opposite — a lean, responsive team that combines architectural design, structural engineering, and creative production under one roof.

Today we design, build, and manage exhibition stands and events across the Middle East and beyond. Our portfolio ranges from compact 18 sqm product showcases to 700 sqm double-decker pavilions for companies like Microsoft. Every project gets the same standard: senior-level attention from the first sketch to the final walkthrough.

We operate from the 29th floor of Marina Plaza in Dubai, but our projects span Saudi Arabia, Abu Dhabi, Oman, and beyond. Our team is small by design — because in this business, speed and accountability matter more than headcount.
