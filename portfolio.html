Featured Project

Microsoft @ LEAP Saudi Arabia

Client: Microsoft

A commanding 700 sqm double-decker pavilion built to serve as Microsoft's centerpiece presence at LEAP in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia. The design was partner-centric: dedicated zones for each Microsoft partner to showcase their solutions, with seamless transitions between demo areas.

The upper deck featured a premium hospitality lounge and private meeting rooms, while the ground floor was engineered for maximum visitor throughput with live demonstration stations, LED video walls, and interactive product displays.

  • 700 sqm footprint — largest build in our portfolio
  • Full double-decker with partner pavilion zones
  • LED video wall integration throughout
  • Premium hospitality and VIP meeting areas
  • Riyadh, Saudi Arabia — international deployment
Product Showcase

Altronix @ Intersec Dubai 2025

Client: Altronix

A 25 sqm stand at stand S2-D18 that punched well above its weight class. The design centered on LED-backlit product displays that showcased Altronix's security and power solutions with theatrical precision.

Despite the compact footprint, the stand included a dedicated meeting area, live product demonstrations, and a visual identity system that made Altronix instantly recognizable from across the hall at Intersec Dubai.

  • 25 sqm optimized layout at stand S2-D18
  • LED-backlit product showcase displays
  • Integrated meeting area within compact footprint
  • Live product demonstration stations
  • Dubai World Trade Centre — Intersec 2025
Artisan Craft

Absolut Skandic @ Light + Intelligent Building ME

Client: Absolut Skandic

A 36 sqm stand designed to showcase Absolut Skandic's handcrafted lighting fixtures in an environment that was itself a lighting experience. The design philosophy was simple: let the product be the hero, and engineer the space to make it glow.

Every surface, angle, and material was selected to complement the warm, artisanal quality of the brand's Scandinavian-designed fixtures. The result was a stand that visitors described as "the most beautiful space on the show floor."

  • 36 sqm — designed around product-as-hero philosophy
  • Handcrafted lighting showcase with curated environments
  • Custom material palette to complement Scandinavian design
  • Atmospheric lighting design with controlled ambience
  • Dubai — Light + Intelligent Building Middle East
Industrial Concept

Spark Logistics @ Breakbulk ME 2025

Client: Spark Logistics

A 60 sqm double-deck stand with a container-inspired design concept that immediately communicated the client's logistics identity. The industrial aesthetic — corrugated panels, steel framing, and branded containers — created a thematic environment that was both functional and visually striking.

The upper deck provided private meeting spaces for client negotiations, while the ground level featured open meeting areas and a prominent reception counter designed for high-volume visitor engagement.

  • 60 sqm double-deck structure with industrial container theme
  • Custom corrugated panel and steel frame construction
  • Upper-level private meeting rooms
  • High-throughput ground-floor reception design
  • Dubai — Breakbulk Middle East 2025
Engineering Excellence

Vanderlande @ Airport Show Dubai

Client: Vanderlande

A 60 sqm double-decker stand featuring an architectural staircase that became the visual centerpiece of Vanderlande's presence at the Airport Show. The design integrated working demonstrations of baggage handling systems with a sophisticated upper-level meeting area.

The engineering challenge was significant: the staircase needed to be both visually dramatic and structurally compliant with Dubai venue safety standards, while the demo equipment required precise floor loading calculations and power distribution planning.

  • 60 sqm double-decker with signature architectural staircase
  • Working baggage handling system demonstration
  • Upper-level executive meeting area
  • Advanced structural engineering for equipment loads
  • Dubai — Airport Show DXB
Premium Compact

IK Group @ ADIPEC

Client: IK Group

An 18 sqm stand that proves you don't need a massive footprint to make a massive impression. The design featured wood-slat paneling with integrated lighting that created a warm, premium feel — a deliberate contrast to the industrial aesthetic of most ADIPEC exhibitors.

Every square centimeter was optimized: product display shelving, a standing meeting table, branded literature storage, and a backlit logo wall — all within 18 sqm. This project exemplifies our ability to deliver maximum impact within tight spatial and budget constraints.

  • 18 sqm — our most space-efficient premium build
  • Wood-slat paneling with integrated accent lighting
  • Optimized layout for product display and meetings
  • Premium material selection within budget parameters
  • Abu Dhabi — ADIPEC (Abu Dhabi International Petroleum Exhibition)
